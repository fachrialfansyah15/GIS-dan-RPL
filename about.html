Road Monitor Palu

Web GIS Road Monitoring System for Palu City, Central Sulawesi

A comprehensive platform designed to monitor, report, and manage road conditions in Palu City. Our system empowers citizens to actively participate in maintaining better infrastructure while providing city officials with real-time data for informed decision-making.

Our Mission

Improving road infrastructure through community engagement and technology

Community Engagement

Empowering citizens to report road issues and participate in infrastructure improvement.

Real-time Monitoring

Providing accurate, up-to-date information about road conditions across Palu City.

Data-Driven Decisions

Supporting city officials with comprehensive data for better infrastructure planning.

Key Features

Comprehensive tools for road monitoring and management

Interactive GIS Map

Real-time visualization of road conditions with detailed mapping capabilities.

Damage Reporting

Easy-to-use forms for citizens to report road damages and maintenance issues.

Dashboard Analytics

Comprehensive dashboard with statistics and progress tracking.

Mobile Responsive

Fully responsive design that works on all devices and screen sizes.

Real-time Notifications

Instant updates on report status and maintenance progress.
